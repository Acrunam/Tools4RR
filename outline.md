#### Jan 31: Know the command line; know your editor

Point-and-click is _not_ reproducible.

Editors:
 - emacs with ESS
 - vim
 - Rstudio
 - textwrangler
 - notepad++
 - Tinn-R

Use relative paths rather than absolute paths, and don't jump out of
the current directory.
